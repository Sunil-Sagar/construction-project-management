Project Blueprint: Multi-Site Civil Contractor Management System
1. Core Objective
To build a high-efficiency internal "Command Center" application (optimized for Laptop/Browser) that replaces manual Google Sheet entries. The system must manage labor attendance, variable wage calculations, multi-site material expenses, and project milestones, providing "on-demand" reporting for senior stakeholders.

2. Labor & Wage Logic (The "Saturday Settlement")
Cycle: Strict Sunday-to-Saturday work week.

Payments: Every Saturday evening.

Wage Calculation: * Base: Daily Rate (from a Master Rate Card) $\times$ Attendance (1.0 or 0.5).

Overtime (OT): Calculated in Hours at a flat hourly rate (e.g., ₹100/hr), not as a fraction of the daily wage.

Total Formula: $(Days \times Rate) + (OT\ Hours \times OT\ Rate) - Advances = Net\ Payable$.

Variable Rates: Rates are individual-specific. If a rate is updated mid-week, the app must apply the old rate to days already worked and the new rate to future days.

Carryover: If a worker is absent on Saturday, their "Net Payable" remains as a balance to be collected on any subsequent day (Monday, Tuesday, etc.), but the "Work Week" itself closes on Saturday.

3. Multi-Site & Global Movement
Global Labor Pool: A single master list of all workers (Masons/Labors/Mestris).

Site Switching: Workers can move between different construction sites. The app must track which site a worker was at on a specific day to ensure costs are allocated to the correct project.

Mestri Management: Treated as daily wage workers (flat rate), with no commission/percentage logic for now.

4. Material & Expense Tracking
Unit Tracking: Focus is on Expense (Value) rather than physical inventory counts.

Vendor Debt: Material deliveries are logged as "Pending" (Debt) and settled later, similar to labor payments.

Price Fluctuations: Material rates (Cement, Sand, etc.) change frequently; the app must allow manual rate entry/overrides for each delivery.

5. Project Progress & Reporting
Milestones: Standardized phases (Excavation $\rightarrow$ Footing $\rightarrow$ Plinth $\rightarrow$ Slab) applied across all sites.

The "Mood-Proof" Report Center: A dedicated dashboard where the user can pick Any Date or Date Range and Any Site to generate:

Daily/Weekly Labor Attendance Reports.

Financial Outflow (Cash vs. UPI) Reports.

Material Consumption & Milestone Status Reports.

Output: Clean, printable layouts or PDFs for clients/Senior Contractors.

6. Technical Architecture (The Stack)
Backend: Google Sheets (7 structured tabs: Sites, Labor Master, Attendance, Advances, Payouts, Materials, Milestones).

Frontend: AppSheet (No-code platform).

User Interface: Stepper-style forms (Step-by-step entry) instead of long scrolling forms.

User Access: Solo operator ("Single Man Army").
